§ 02
Companions in this bed
1 match
✓
Tomato+Basil
Classic pairing — basil masks the tomato's scent from hornworms, deters whiteflies, and is said to improve the fruit's flavor when grown alongside.
✓
Lettuce+Tomato
The tomato's canopy shades the lettuce, slowing bolt and extending the harvest into early summer. Mutually beneficial in this bed's south-facing exposure.
!
Avoid Fennel in this bed
Fennel is allelopathic — its root secretions inhibit growth in most companions, especially tomatoes and beans. Plant it elsewhere.
